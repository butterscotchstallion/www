Information Gathering and Usage

What information is required for registration with services?

You will need a valid email address to complete registration. This address is used to help you recover your account if you misplace your password. This address is hidden from others by default through the NickServ's HideMail option.

Does freenode collect data about public or private channel messages? If so, what data and how long is it kept?

Public and private messages are not logged at the network level. However, other clients connected to the network can log public and private messages.
